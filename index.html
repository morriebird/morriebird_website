Hello, I'm morriebird! (she/he)

I'm a self-taught artist who takes a lot of inspiration from early edition RPG books and comics like Prince Valiant and Conan the Barbarian. I primarily work digitally with a pencil-like brush reminiscent of my cheap BiC mechanical pencil from when I started on paper.

I live in the Pacific Northwest. My background includes environmental restoration, coding, technical writing, and instructional design.

Hire me! I am looking for full-time or part-time work.

Current Work

The Meanderings of Moreven the Mage

A comic I personally want made.

A low fantasy episodic story following Moreven, a traveling mage who aids people dealing with unusual problems.

My inspiration for this story comes from works like Ursula K. Le Guin's Earthsea, Garth Nix's Abhorsen Series, Jack Vance's Cugel's Saga, Yuki Urushibara's Mushishi, and George Miller's Mad Max.

I'm making progress! Updates will be on ko-fi.
