Hello, I'm morriebird! (she/they)

I'm a self-taught artist who takes a lot of inspiration from early edition RPG books and B/W comics like Berserk and Conan. I primarily work digitally with a pencil-like brush reminiscent of my cheap BiC mechanical pencil from when I started on paper.

I live in the Pacific Northwest. My background includes environmental restoration, coding, and technical writing. Now I am a full-time freelance artist looking for work!

Hire me! I'm available to work on long term projects.

Current Work

The Meanderings of Moreven the Mage

A low fantasy episodic story following Moreven, a traveling mage who aids people dealing with unusual problems.

My inspiration for this story comes from works like Ursula K. Le Guin's Earthsea, Garth Nix's Abhorsen Series, Jack Vance's Cugel's Saga, Yuki Urushibara's Mushishi, and George Miller's Mad Max.

This comic is a work in progress! Please join my mailing list, support me on Ko-fi, or follow me on social media to get updates and be part of the creative process.

Past Work

Sanctimonious Slimes vs Expired Epicures

An adventure written by Nick LS Whelan (Papers and Pencils, Miscreated Creatures) brimming with dynamic factions and weird magic. Delve into the dungeon and face an ennui afflicted medusa, holier-than-thou slime people, and hedonistic skeletons!

I illustrated the cover and the majority of interior pieces.

Eel Folk Aristocrat

A cover for Eel Folk Aristocrat, a DURF Archetype written by Kris Redmond. Created for DURF JAM 2023.
